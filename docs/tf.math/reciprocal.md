

Computes the reciprocal of x element-wise.

**别名** : [ `tf.compat.v1.math.reciprocal` ](/api_docs/python/tf/math/reciprocal), [ `tf.compat.v1.reciprocal` ](/api_docs/python/tf/math/reciprocal), [ `tf.compat.v2.math.reciprocal` ](/api_docs/python/tf/math/reciprocal)

```
 tf.math.reciprocal(    x,    name=None) 
```

I.e., \(y = 1 / x\).

#### 参数：
- **`x`** : A  `Tensor` . Must be one of the following types:  `bfloat16` ,  `half` ,  `float32` ,  `float64` ,  `int32` ,  `int64` ,  `complex64` ,  `complex128` .
- **`name`** : A name for the operation (optional).


#### 返回：
A  `Tensor` . Has the same type as  `x` .
